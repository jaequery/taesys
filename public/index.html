About Us


Building the Next Generation of Tech Leaders

We TAESYS believe that with today's globally connected world, it makes little difference that the companies young korean programmers work for are based thousands of miles away with a most fastest Internet connection in the world, a world-class developer can add value remotely from wherever in the world they happen to be.

In the next ten years, there will be 1.3M software development jobs created and only 400,000 domestic computer science graduates to fill them. Korea, meanwhile, is home to the largest untapped talent pool and home of the highest concentration of IT users in the world.

We founded Taesys in 2018 to build a network of technology leaders in Asia and bridge the divide between the U.S and the Asia's tech sectors. Leveraging a proven, data-driven approach, we build high-performing engineering teams with Korea’s top developers to help companies overcome the tech talent shortage and build better products, faster.

Our mission is to solve the developer shortage with tech workers from Asia and to adavance human potential by powering today's developers and investing in tomorrow's leaders.
